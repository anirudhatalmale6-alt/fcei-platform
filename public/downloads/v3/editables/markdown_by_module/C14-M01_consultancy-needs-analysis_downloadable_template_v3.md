# C14-M01 - Consultancy Needs Analysis - Professional Downloadable Toolkit

**Aligned course:** C14 - FCEI Consultancy, Workshops and Institutional Implementation  
**FCEI focus:** consultancy implementation  
**Intended users:** school leaders, owners, managers, coordinators and institutional improvement teams  
**SKU:** FCEI-DL-C14-M01-CONSULTANCY_NEEDS_ANALYS

## Short explanation
This downloadable toolkit supports Consultancy Needs Analysis within FCEI Consultancy, Workshops and Institutional Implementation. It helps participants translate Finnish education principles into a reusable professional artefact, not simply complete a reading activity.

## OKM / Ministry of Education and Culture framing
The resource connects classroom, leadership and institutional development to the wider Ministry of Education and Culture policy context: education improvement should be planned as part of system development, quality, inclusion, research-informed practice and public responsibility.

## EDUFI-aligned framing
The resource uses EDUFI-aligned wording by treating curriculum and learning design as a common foundation that supports equality while allowing local adaptation. Participants connect national-level principles to local curriculum, school culture, pedagogy and learner experience.

## FINEEC-benchmarked framing
The resource uses FINEEC-benchmarked wording by applying enhancement-led development: participation, trust, learning, appreciation, strengths, development targets and impact. Evidence is used to improve practice, not to blame individuals.

## Key terminology and definitions
- **Principle:** the educational value or idea being made visible in practice
- **Problem:** the real learning, teaching, leadership, curriculum or institutional challenge being addressed
- **Tool:** the template, routine, planning document, dialogue structure or evidence instrument used to respond to the challenge
- **Evidence:** visible proof that the action has been applied, reviewed and connected to improvement
- **Improvement Cycle:** identify priority, plan action, implement, collect evidence, reflect, adjust and decide the next step
- **Enhancement Led Development:** a participatory, trust-based improvement approach focused on learning, strengths, development targets and impact

## Meaning in practice
In practice, participants use the toolkit to identify one priority related to Consultancy Needs Analysis, choose a manageable action, complete the professional template, collect evidence and decide what should improve next.

## Editable template fields
- Context and role
- Priority or problem statement
- EDUFI-aligned principle in action
- FINEEC-benchmarked evidence focus
- Action steps
- Evidence source
- Stakeholder or learner voice
- Reflection and next improvement decision

## Evidence requirement
needs analysis, stakeholder voice record, implementation timeline and impact reflection

## Model completed example
A participant notices that consultancy needs analysis is discussed in general terms but is not yet visible in daily practice. They complete the toolkit by selecting one priority, designing a focused action, collecting one piece of evidence, and writing a next-step improvement decision.

## Reflection and improvement prompt
What changed in practice after using this Consultancy Needs Analysis resource? What evidence supports your judgement? What should be continued, adapted, scaled or redesigned?

## Certificate evidence rule
The downloadable evidence is complete when the participant submits a filled Consultancy Needs Analysis - Professional Downloadable Toolkit, includes evidence connected to needs analysis, stakeholder voice record, implementation timeline and impact reflection, and writes one improvement decision aligned to the FCEI Finnish Teacher Training Development Framework.
